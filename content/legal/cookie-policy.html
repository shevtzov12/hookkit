COOKIE POLICY
Last updated July 06, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how ALEKSEI SHEVTSOV (doing business as HookKit, "we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit https://hookkit-phi.vercel.app ("Website").

HookKit uses essential cookies only — for sign-in (Clerk) and security (Cloudflare). We do not use advertising, analytics, or marketing cookies. We do not use a Termly "Cookie Preference Center"; you control cookies through your browser.

Essential cookies (Clerk — authentication)

Set when you sign in to the dashboard. Clerk may add an instance suffix to cookie names (for example __clerk_db_jwt_*, __client_uat_*).
Name Provider Purpose Duration
__client_uat Clerk · hookkit-phi.vercel.app User authentication token (Clerk) As set by Clerk (long-lived)
__clerk_db_jwt Clerk · hookkit-phi.vercel.app Session / JWT for signed-in users ~12 months
__clerk_redirect_count Clerk · hookkit-phi.vercel.app Sign-in redirect flow Very short (minutes or less)

Essential cookies (Cloudflare — security)

Used by Clerk and Cloudflare to protect sign-in and (when enabled) Turnstile on public forms. Termly scans sometimes show a Clerk auth hostname as "Provider" for a Cloudflare cookie — the service is still Cloudflare, not HookKit.
Name Provider Purpose Duration
__cf_bm Cloudflare Bot management / security on protected pages ~30 minutes
_cfuvid Cloudflare Trusted traffic / performance & security Session

Local storage (not cookies)

Name Provider Purpose Duration

How to control cookies

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc.). Blocking essential cookies may prevent you from signing in or using Turnstile-protected forms.

Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy when we change auth or security providers. The "Last updated" date at the top shows the latest revision.
